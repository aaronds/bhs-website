Before contacting us, please check this short FAQ in case it answers your question.

Are you still open? / Are your open evenings still on? / Can I come take a look?
Yes! We hold regular open evenings throughout the year, where you can have a tour of the facilities and become a member if you wish. Full details here.
I only need to use this one tool, could I just quickly…?
Sorry, due to our membership-based structure and insurance stipulations, our facilities are only available to our members, and cannot be hired or used on a one-off basis.
I need to start my project urgently! Can I join and start working immediately?

Before you can join, you need to come to an open evening and take a tour. You can then sign up and become a member straight away, if you want to!

But please note that some of our equipment also requires you to undertake a safety induction before you can use it. These inductions are run by volunteers in their spare time, and can take a little while to arrange. Because of this, we might not be best choice if you need immediate use of the facilities.

Can you fix my…?
This is not a service we aim to provide, you may be better off trying one of Bristol's numerous Repair Cafes.
